# Bus-Management
>The system takes information of students, buses, drivers, routes etc as input source and attempts to maintain the bus services. 
>It allows flexibility during processes like adding, deleting and updating of records for the admin. 
>Students can view records of drivers, buses and their routes. They can also give suggestions and voice out their complaints regarding the transportation system.
